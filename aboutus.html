MISSION

Waste handling has been a major concern for the society for a very long time now. Designing a smart waste collection system that allows citizens to segregate the various types of solid waste they want to dispose and the small scale startups to efficiently collect the same for recycling purposes

SOLUTION

  • Our team proposes to develop a one step platform where waste producers and waste consumers can directly connect with each other for the proper disposal of waste produce. The main idea is to give a boost to the upcoming eco friendly companies which transform waste into useful products.
  • In our model, the waste generated in the households will be purchased by the small scale industries. This will benefit both the industies as well as households.
  • Earlier they used to buy waste from government at high prices but buying them from households directly will cut their cost of raw materials.
  • If we observe the household's end, they will receive some money out of their waste.

VISION

  • To Dispose Waste from every household.
  • To ease the process of Recycling.
  • To avoid Waste Spillage.
  • To facilitate easier availability of raw products for small scale industies.
